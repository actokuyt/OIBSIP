Fela Anikulapo Kuti

"Music must awaken people to do their duties as citizens, and act."

Fela Kuti, best known as the pioneer of Afrobeat. Fela used his music to critique political corruption and social injustice in Nigeria. Despite facing adversity, Fela's influential music legacy and activism have left an enduring impact on African and global music and social movements.
